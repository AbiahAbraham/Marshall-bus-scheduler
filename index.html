The Marshall Community Transit Bus Routes. Schedules are subject to change due to staffing.

Follow the rules while riding or you can be suspended from using the community bus.

The Red Route

This route is $1 for a ride and is available every day of the week.

The Blue Route

This route is $1 for a ride and is available Monday-Friday.

Dial-a-Ride

This route is available every day of the week.
$2.00 per person when scheduled before 4:30pm.
$2.50 per person when scheduled after 4:30pm.
$1.00 per each child ages 3-12 years old.
Free for children 2 years old and younger.
